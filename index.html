Our Flame-Grilled Story

At BurnIT Corner, we believe exceptional food begins with fire. Our passion for authentic smokehouse flavours meets the vibrant spirit of Hammanskraal, Gauteng.

Founded in 2020 by two friends with a shared love for authentic South African braai culture, BurnIT Corner has grown from a local favorite to a premier dining destination. We combine traditional smoking techniques with modern culinary innovation.

Our commitment is to source the finest local ingredients, supporting Gauteng farmers and producers. Each dish is crafted to deliver a bold, unforgettable experience that honours our South African heritage.

From our signature slow-smoked ribs to our gourmet burgers, every bite tells a story of dedication, quality, and local pride.
